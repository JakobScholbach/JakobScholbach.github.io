Compute the following complex numbers in the form $z = a+ib$:

- $(1-i)(2+i)$

- $(1+i)^3 (:=(1+i)(1+i)(1+i))$

- $i^{89}$

- $e^{-i\pi}$

- $3 (\cos (\pi / 2) + i \sin (\pi / 2))$

- $\frac{\sqrt 3 + \sqrt 2 i}{\sqrt 2 - \sqrt 3 i}$

Depict these complex numbers on the complex plane.
